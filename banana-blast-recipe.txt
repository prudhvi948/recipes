Ingredients:
 -2 bananas
 -2 tablespoons brown sugar
 -1 cup milk
 -1/4 cup water
 -8 cubes ice

Directions:
 1. In a blender, combine bananas and milk.
 2. Pulse until bananas are chopped.
 3. Pour in water and brown sugar.
 4. Blend until smooth. 
 5. Toss in the ice cubes and blend until smooth.
 6. Pour into 4 glasses and serve immediately.
